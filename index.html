About Me

Hi, I'm Raoul and I'm a Web-Developer trainee at BeCode.
This might sound a lot like all the other trainees and students you have seen portolio's or CV's from. Hence this page.
In one of my internships, we worked on a project that involved the Aldous Huxley novel 'A Brave New World'. The project invited people to reflect on their #bravenewworld in which, in contrast to the gloomy predicament that is set in the book, they explain how they see our world that is challenging people to think differently to the benefit of a new and better world. For me, this meant learning to think differently about aspects of our daily life so we can do things more sustainably. Professionally, I carry this ideology in every room I walk, and try to apply it wherever I can. My goal is to develop myself in this growing digital world as a Backend Developer. I want to contribute to solutions and make our society more sustainable. I want to put my skills to use in a changing world. I think your company could benefit from my skills as a developer and out-of-the-box thinking. Should you have any projects that I might be an asset for feel free to contact me.
